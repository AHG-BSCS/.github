**Al Hans Gaming (AHG)** is founded by *John Andrei Valdez*, *Jhondale Arandia*, and *Andrew James Bactad* during their first software development project on June 2023. The group is based on Laguna State Polytechnic University – San Pablo City Campus (LSPU-SPCC) and composed of computer science students. This served as the platform to share their open-sourced application projects that the members collaborated throughout their study (or even in the future).

**Fun Fact:** The name `Al Hans` is inspired by one of their teacher's name. Even though there is `Gaming` in the group name, the group doesn't necessarily develop games or stream gameplay.
